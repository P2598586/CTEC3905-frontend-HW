Services

Make Donation

Send your donations now to I T Outreach Ministries, Inc so that a person in need can be helped with your money. Your donations are in safe hands and will be going to their rightful owners.

Become A Volunteer

if you can't donate, you can even become a volunteer and help I T outreach ministries find needy families and provide them with necessities by helping us collect donations and deliver stuffs to deserving people.

Give Scholarship

You can even arrange a scholarship for a deserving student who can’t manage to pay the fee or bear other educational expenses. Get in touch with I T Outreach Ministries and find out to whom you can offer a scholarship and how that student deserves it.

About

Less is more work

We are a non-profit organization, 501c3 helping families and children internationally. All the donations sent to us are used to help needy people.

Our programs: Feed the homeless, educational nonprofits for young students in Haiti, Environmental health care for children and low-income families, Supporting young students with their school fees all over the country.
